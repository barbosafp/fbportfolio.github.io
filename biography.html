BIOGRAPHY

Fabiana Barbosa is a videographer from Sao Paulo, Brazil. Although she has formerly graduated in Biology, her passion for visual arts and willingness to learn provided the knowledge and hands-on experience she needed to start getting occasional jobs in video production and photography in 2012, while still working as a biologist.
In 2016, Fabiana opted to dedicate herself exclusively to the videographer career. She attended short courses in camera, set design, lighting, and editing. In 2017, she moved to Canada aiming to improve her English language skills and get further education on visual arts. Currently, she is taking the Video Production Program at Durham College, Oshawa, Ontario, Canada.
